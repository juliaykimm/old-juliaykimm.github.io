BUCKWHEAT CHOCOLATE COOKIE

back to home page

DETAILS

  • YIELDS: 12 - 14
  • TIME: 30 - 40 min (includes baking

    • INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups buckwheat flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 cups light brown sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon strongly brewed espresso or coffee>
  • powder sugar and/or salt for sprinkling

    • STEPS

    1. Whisk all your dry ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
    2. In another bowl, whisk your sugars and butter until fluffy.
    3. Once fluffy, slowly start mixing in your dry ingredients. Whip until no more dry spots can be seen.
    4. Add in your chocolate chips and espresso until everything is combined.
    5. Por the dough out onto a clean surface and shape it into a log roughly 10 inches long and 2 inches tall.
    6. Cut the log into ~12 discs and place in a prepared baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    7. dust with powdere sugar and/or salt.
    8. Bake at 375° for 12-15 min until browned and cracked at the tops. Flip halfway if your oven bakes unevenly.

    NOTES

  • You can also use salted butter. Just make sure to always add less salt.
  • It is important to watch them while they bake because you don't want to over cook these.
