LEMON RING CAKE

back to home page

DETAILS

  • YIELDS: 1 cake, serves 1 - 12 people
  • TIME: 1 hr 20 minutes (includes baking)

    • INGREDIENTS

    for the cake:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 teaspooon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablesoons lemon zest

    • for the glaze:

  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup sugar

    • STEPS

    1. Butter a 9 inch tube pan and dust with flour.
    2. Sift together your flour, baking powder, and salt and set aside.
    3. Cream butter and sugar until combined and fluffy. Then beat in your eggs one at a time.
    4. Fold in your dry ingredients and milk alternating between the two.
    5. Fold in lemon zest.
    6. Pour the batter into your pan and smooth out the top with a spatula or by shaking it back and forth.
    7. Bake at 325° for ~1 hour. Test for doneness with a toothpick.
    8. While the cake is baking, make the glaze by heating your sugar and lemon juice over medium low heat until the sugar is dissolved. Cover until the cake is done baking.
    9. Whent he cake is done, flip it out onto a cooling rack and pour the glaze over top (or use a pastry brush), making sure you cover the while cake.
    10. Cool the cake completely and enjoy!

    NOTES

  • This is the easiest cake recipe I know, it never fails me so if you're new to baking I recommend this one.
  • It is important to beat the eggs in ONE AT A TIME so the batter has time to thicken without being bombarded with eggs.
  • I highly recommend getting a microplane for the lemon zest otherwise life will be very hard trying to get it off the lemons easily.
  • Sometimes the cake cooks faster than an hour, around 55 minutes, so make sure to check in it with a toothpick.
  • Make sure to let the cake cool completely before eating it. You have to give it time for the glaze to harden, which gives it it's nice nice crunchy sweet crust.
